# OreSpawn x Terraria progression blueprint

This document is the first practical blueprint for folding OreSpawn content into Chaotic Dimensions without letting it erase the mod's own identity.

The main idea is simple:

- Chaotic Dimensions keeps its own endgame identity.
- Most OreSpawn boss pages become elite mobs or natural minibosses in Terraria.
- Only the biggest Apex OreSpawn encounters stay as full bosses with music, presentation, and progression weight.
- Ant, termite, and butterfly travel should be reinterpreted as expeditions, rifts, or staged biome events instead of literal Minecraft-style teleports.

## Core adaptation rules

- Keep `Crystaline Devourer` as the first true Chaotic Dimensions post-Moon Lord gate.
- Make `Kraken`, `Mobzilla`, `The King`, and `The Queen` the main OreSpawn official bosses.
- Everything else from the OreSpawn boss pages should default to miniboss or elite-spawn status unless future testing proves it deserves full-boss treatment.
- Reuse vanilla Terraria materials where direct OreSpawn duplicates would create bloat.
- For name collisions like `Titanium`, prefer a distinct Terraria-facing name such as `Titanium Nugget`, `OreSpawn Titanium`, or another custom label.

## Recommended official boss order

1. `Crystaline Devourer` - `Post Moon Lord`
2. `Future Chaotic Boss 1` - `Post Crystaline Devourer`
3. `Future Chaotic Boss 2` - `Post Future Chaotic Boss 1`
4. `Future Chaotic Boss 3` - `Post Future Chaotic Boss 2`
5. `Kraken` - `Post Future Chaotic Boss Trio`
6. `Mobzilla` - `Post Kraken`
7. `The King` - `Post Mobzilla`
8. `The Queen` - `Post The King`

This preserves the Chaotic Dimensions endgame lane exactly the way you described it: OreSpawn apex bosses only arrive after your own post-Devour trio.

- Put your most explosive soundtrack and presentation budget here if you want the Orespawn branch to end with a real payoff.

## Dimension and event priority order

Implementation priority should be:

1. Overworld fauna and early minibosses
2. Mining Expedition
3. Village Siege / Rainbow Rift
4. Kraken lane
5. Crystal Expedition
6. Danger Expedition
7. Chaos Rift sandbox
8. the future Chaotic post-Devour trio
9. Kraken
10. Mobzilla
11. Utopia endgame

This order gives you content across the whole Terraria run instead of locking everything to the end.

## Big Bertha lane

`Big Bertha` should be your major OreSpawn craft chase before the official `Mobzilla` fight, even if the true boss version of `Mobzilla` now sits much later in your combined endgame.

Recommended Terraria placement:

- Parts begin across Stages 5 to 7
- Full craft should happen around `Post Plantera` to `Post Golem`
- The weapon should feel like the "I farmed the whole OreSpawn ecosystem" reward

## Final recommendation

If you want the healthiest long-term version of this crossover, do not import OreSpawn as one giant dump of mobs.

Instead:

- spread its mobs across the full Terraria campaign
- reserve only the apex encounters as full bosses
- use expeditions and staged events to organize the chaos
- let `Crystaline Devourer` stay the first true bridge into the mod's custom endgame

That gives you `OreSpawn + Chaotic Dimensions` instead of `Chaotic Dimensions swallowed by OreSpawn`.
