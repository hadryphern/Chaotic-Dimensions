[h1]Chaotic Dimensions[/h1]

[b]Chaotic Dimensions[/b] is an original Terraria / tModLoader mod focused on handcrafted post-Moon Lord content, crystal-cosmic atmosphere and cinematic encounters.

This project is being built as a larger custom universe over time, with multiple bosses, original progression, custom visuals and memorable fights.

[h2]Current Content[/h2]

The current public version introduces [b]Crystaline Devour[/b], the first official boss of the project.

Included in this release:

- Custom boss encounter
- Intro / title presentation
- Custom boss bar
- Custom battle sky and color treatment
- Arena-based fight structure
- Original weapons, accessories and progression rewards
- Crystaline Devour modular armor set

[h2]Project Direction[/h2]

Chaotic Dimensions is not planned as a single-boss mod. Crystaline Devour is the first official step of a much larger vision involving new bosses, new equipment, new systems and original dimensional content.

[h2]Status[/h2]

The mod is in active development.
More bosses, items, mechanics and content expansions are planned for future updates.
